Spicy Tuna Recipe

For when your tuna just isn't fiery enough. Now with canned tuna!

Ingredients:

  • ¾ can tuna
  • 2 tbsp mayonaise
  • 1 tbsp mustard
  • ½ tbsp red pepper
  • ½ tbsp honey
  • ½ tbsp canola oil

Steps:

  1. In a bowl, mix all ingredients together.
  2. Enjoy in sushi, or on rice.
