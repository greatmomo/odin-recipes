Chili Oil Recipe

A little bit of heat and a whole lot of flavor for all your favorite dishes.

Ingredients:

  • 4 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tbsp chili flakes
  • ¼ tbsp cayenne pepper
  • ½ tbsp ginger, sliced
  • 1 small clove garlic, paste
  • pinch of salt

Steps:

  1. Place spices in a heat proof bowl.
  2. Heat oil in a pan until it moves like water.
  3. Add oil to spices and mix 3-4 minutes.
  4. Store in a jar in the refrigerator for up to 2 months.
